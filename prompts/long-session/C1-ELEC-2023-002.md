## Workstream B — Ballot Accounting

### Goal

Determine how the visible organization-vote sum of `26,091` relates to official ballot accounting.

### Required Fields

Acquire authoritative values where available for:

- registered electorate;
- ballots cast;
- valid organization votes;
- null votes;
- blank votes;
- challenged or other categories;
- printed totals;
- participation;
- abstention.

### Calculation Policy

- `organization_vote_sum = sum(verified organization rows)`.
- `valid_vote_total` must be printed by an official source or labeled `DERIVED`.
- `ballots_cast` is not equivalent to valid organization votes.
- `participation_rate = ballots_cast / registered_electorate` only when both inputs match year, election, municipality, and territorial scope.
- `abstention_rate = 1 - participation_rate` only under the same conditions.
- Residual categories may not be inferred unless a complete official accounting identity supports the derivation; label any such value `DERIVED`.

### Reconciliation Outcomes

Use exactly one:

- `RECONCILED`
- `RECONCILED_WITH_DOCUMENTED_CATEGORIES`
- `PARTIAL_RECONCILIATION`
- `NOT_RECONCILED`

Every equation must show values, status, and source.
