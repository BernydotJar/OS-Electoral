## Intake Checklist

- Source name.
- Absolute path or repository location.
- Date collected or last modified.
- Territory covered.
- Population or segment covered.
- Method.
- Limitations.
- Evidence class.
- Reliability assessment.
- Decision supported.
- Review status.
