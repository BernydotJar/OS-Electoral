## Objective

Persist one campaign-scoped operational roadmap and one append-only sequence of Daily War Room snapshots that connect phases, milestones, workstreams, dependencies, ready tasks, blockers, decisions, owners, follow-up and learning without executing political or external actions.

## Bounded model

- phases with ordered date windows;
- milestones with completion criteria and human owner role;
- workstreams with one accountable team role;
- tasks with dependencies, owner role, state, due date and evidence refs;
- deterministic dependency graph validation and critical path;
- blockers with severity, owner and resolution condition;
- decisions required with human role, options, deadline and state;
- daily brief derived from exact roadmap version;
- follow-up items and learning notes;
- append-only War Room snapshots bound to roadmap version;
- exact authorization, idempotency, optimistic concurrency, audit and internal no-effect outbox.

## Exact authorization purposes

```text
Create campaign operations roadmap
Review campaign operations roadmap
Maintain campaign operations roadmap
Create daily campaign war room snapshot
Review daily campaign war room snapshot
```

## Acceptance criteria

1. A reversible migration adds one tenant/campaign roadmap and append-only War Room snapshots under forced RLS.
2. Roadmap creation requires an existing Team Workspace.
3. IDs and references remain inside the same aggregate; cycles and self-dependencies are rejected.
4. Ready tasks are derived only when every dependency is complete and no open blocker targets the task.
5. Critical path is deterministic and bounded; no task is silently omitted.
6. Every active workstream, milestone, task, blocker and required decision has an explicit human owner role from Team Workspace.
7. Daily snapshots bind to exact roadmap version and date and are append-only.
8. A snapshot exposes priorities, ready work, blockers, decisions due, follow-up and learning without changing roadmap state.
9. Writes are exact-authorized, idempotent, versioned, audited and atomically produce only an internal no-effect outbox event.
10. No task, recommendation or snapshot creates authority, contacts citizens, publishes, spends, mobilizes or invokes an external provider.
11. API errors are sanitized and adapter scope/version drift fails closed.
12. PostgreSQL proves forced RLS, cross-tenant denial, replay, race handling and immutable snapshots.
13. Frontend validates the entire projection and presents a read-only ES/EN roadmap and War Room brief.
14. Production remains `BLOCKED`.
