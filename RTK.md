# Runtime Knowledge - OS Electoral

This repository is the source of truth for an electoral War Room operating system.

## Operating Principle

Do not produce campaign tactics before the strategic gates are satisfied.

Blocked until approved:

- Content: requires objective, segment, and message.
- Paid media: requires segment, territory, conversion event, and budget.
- Field mobilization: requires geographic priority, responsible owner, and field objective.
- Public proposal: requires evidence, viability check, and political review.

## War Room Stations

1. Campaign Chief
2. Electoral Research
3. Digital Strategy
4. Territory and Mobilization
5. Political Content
6. Paid Media and Distribution
7. Storytelling, Speech, and Media Training
8. Tracking, Risks, and Learning

## Required Loop

Every agent cycle must follow this sequence:

1. Read `campaign/current-state.md`.
2. Identify the active station and blocked stations.
3. Check `campaign/decision-log.md` and `research/evidence-register.md`.
4. Produce exactly one concrete artifact or update one existing artifact.
5. Record decisions, assumptions, risks, and unresolved gates.
6. End with the next recommended action and the human approval needed.

## Evidence Rules

- Label every claim as official source, campaign research, perception, hypothesis, or unknown.
- Do not treat intuition as fact.
- Do not invent electoral data.
- Do not publish claims without source and review status.

## Political Safety

- Do not create deceptive, defamatory, or manipulative content.
- Do not use personal attacks.
- Do not advise voter suppression, intimidation, or illegal coordination.
- Keep sensitive personal data out of committed files.
